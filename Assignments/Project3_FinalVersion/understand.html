Human Trafficking in the United States

In the U.S., at least 16,658 victims of human trafficking were identified.
42% of these victims were brought into trafficking by a member of their own family.

Human Trafficking in Communities

Many people believe that human trafficking does not affect their community. This simply isn't true.
In the State of Utah, a relatively small community, over 470 victims of human trafficking have been identified in the last 15 years.
