Shelter Location

Victims of domestic violence are more at risk. They can find shelter here:

The Refuge, Utah

The Refuge is a shelter for victims of domestic violence and sexual assault. These people are at higher risk for trafficking. Knowing where the shelter is can be useful knowledge for giving at-risk people the help they need. They offer temporary housing, guidance, legal assistance, and more.
